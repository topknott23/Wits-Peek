# WITS-PEEK

A super fast, no-BS way to check your CIT-U grades when the official WITS portal is acting up (which is basically always).

### Why I made this
CITUWITS is slow, laggy, and crashes whenever you actually need it. This tool skips all the unnecessary stuff and just gives you your grades immediately. No waiting, no lag, just your data. It's basically my personal shortcut to keep my sanity during finals week.
